Anibry

Anibry is an online web based animation library that can used all kinds of web pages to make website Anibry is a library that allows beginner developers perform basic web animation using normal languages lively,engaging, and interactive. We started in 2022 as a team under Zuri project phase, and we thought we were headed to the promised land with that business model.

Meet the team

Our team comprises of creative minds of designers and developers from all over different states of Nigeria with a goal to make website interactive and attractive.

Our Story

We're a group of randomly selected students that passed through the Zuri x 14G training session from July to August of 2022 and was given a task to make this as a passing out project task. With our able coodinators and mentor assigned to us during the training and project phrase, we are thankful and appreciative to the host, organizers and sponsors for this awesome privilege to be on this project.
